![Google Sign in Process](https://github.com/UAlberta-CMPUT401/CS-and-Law/blob/refactor/docs/images/Frame%202.png)

### From the Control Center, go to the settings screen, then the contacts screen to add contacts that alerts will be sent to in an emergency
![Add contacts](https://github.com/UAlberta-CMPUT401/CS-and-Law/blob/refactor/docs/images/Frame%203.png)

### From the Control Center, swipe right on the primary button to send alerts to contacts
![Add contacts](https://github.com/UAlberta-CMPUT401/CS-and-Law/blob/refactor/docs/images/Frame%205.png)

### From the Control Center, swipe left on the primary button to start a recording that will be automatically uploaded to the user's Google Drive
![Add contacts](https://github.com/UAlberta-CMPUT401/CS-and-Law/blob/refactor/docs/images/Frame%206.png)

### From the Control Center, long press on the primary button to perform both actions of sending alerts and starting a recording
![Add contacts](https://github.com/UAlberta-CMPUT401/CS-and-Law/blob/refactor/docs/images/Frame%207.png)

### From the Control Center, press on the info button to access the Know Your Rights screen
